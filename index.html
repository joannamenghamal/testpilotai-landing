Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from uptime monitoring?

Uptime monitors just check if your site is online. TestPilotAI actually tests your checkout flow end-to-end — adding products to cart, proceeding to checkout, and verifying the payment form loads. We catch bugs that uptime monitors miss.

What if my checkout changes or I update my theme?

Our tests are self-healing. We use multiple strategies to find elements (text content, ARIA labels, semantic analysis), so when your theme updates, tests automatically adapt. No maintenance required from you.
