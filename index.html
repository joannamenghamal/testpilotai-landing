FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to write any code or scripts?
No. TestPilotAI requires zero code, zero Playwright knowledge, and zero configuration. Paste your store URL, set a check frequency, and you're live in under 60 seconds.
How is this different from uptime monitoring tools?
Uptime monitoring checks if your site loads — it has no idea if checkout actually works. Your site can be 100% "up" while checkout is completely broken. TestPilotAI runs a real browser through your full purchase flow: product discovery, variant selection, add to cart, checkout page, payment form — everything a real customer would need to actually buy.
Does it execute real transactions?
No. TestPilotAI runs a real browser through your storefront — discovering a product, selecting a variant, adding to cart, and reaching checkout — without completing a live payment. Nothing touches your payment gateway.
Where does it run?
Against your live production storefront, not staging. It catches regressions that only surface under real storefront conditions — after a theme update, app install, or payment config change.
What does a health score mean?
Each store gets a health score based on recent run history. A score of 100% means every validation has passed. Degraded and failed runs both affect the score — giving you an at-a-glance view of store health over time.
What happens when an issue is detected?
You get an immediate alert with the status, what was missing, a screenshot, and an HTML snapshot — everything you need to debug fast, before a customer notices.
