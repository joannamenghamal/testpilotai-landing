Technical FAQ

Do I need to write any code or scripts?

No. TestPilotAI requires zero code, zero Playwright knowledge, and zero configuration. Paste your store URL, set a check frequency, and you're live in under 60 seconds.

How is this different from Checkly or Datadog?

Checkly and Datadog require you to write and maintain Playwright test scripts — one per store. TestPilotAI needs only a URL. For agencies managing 10, 20, or 50 stores, that's the difference between hours of setup and 60 seconds.

Does it execute real transactions?

No. The engine validates deterministic checkout signals — cart, variant, checkout page structure — without completing live payments.

Where does it run?

Against your live production storefront, not staging. It catches regressions that only surface under real storefront conditions — after a theme update, app install, or payment config change.

What does a health score mean?

Each store gets a health score based on recent run history. A score of 100% means every validation has passed. Degraded runs (partial checkout issues) and failures both affect the score, giving you an at-a-glance view of store health over time.

What happens when an issue is detected?

You get an immediate email alert with the status (degraded or fail), what was missing, a screenshot, and an HTML snapshot — everything you need to debug fast.
